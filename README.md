# MUNSMART

### Your Smart Guide to Model United Nations.

MUNSMART is a free educational platform created to help beginner Model United Nations delegates understand MUN in a clear, practical, and accessible way.

The platform brings together preparation guidance, public speaking support, parliamentary procedure, crisis committees, resolution writing, downloadable resources, and beginner-friendly explanations in one place.

🌐 Live website: https://munsmart.lovable.app/

---

## Mission

MUNSMART’s mission is to make Model United Nations more understandable, accessible, and enjoyable for beginner delegates around the world.

The platform was created to reduce confusion, build confidence, and support students as they prepare for their first MUN experiences.

---

## Why MUNSMART?

Many beginner delegates arrive at their first conferences feeling nervous, confused, or unprepared.

MUNSMART was created to solve that problem by offering a structured, simple, and practical learning space where students can understand how MUN works before entering committee.

Instead of searching through scattered resources, beginner delegates can use MUNSMART as a central guide for learning the essentials of Model United Nations.

## Vision

MUNSMART aims to become a trusted educational platform for students who want to learn Model United Nations and participate with greater confidence.

The long-term vision is to make MUN preparation accessible to students, schools, clubs, and conferences around the world.
