# MUNSMART Master Roadmap

---

## Vision

Become the world's leading educational platform for Model United Nations.

---

## Mission

Provide free, practical and high-quality educational resources that help every delegate, regardless of experience or country, become a better Model United Nations participant.

---

## Core Values

- Accessibility
- Educational Excellence
- Global Collaboration
- Practical Learning
- Innovation
- Community

---

# Strategic Pillars

## Education

Build the most complete free MUN learning platform.

---

## Technology

Develop innovative digital tools that improve delegate preparation.

---

## Community

Create the largest international community of MUN delegates.

---

## Partnerships

Collaborate with schools, universities and international conferences.

---

## AI

Integrate Artificial Intelligence to help delegates learn faster.

---

## Global Growth

Expand MUNSMART to every region where Model United Nations exists.

---

# Long-Term Goal

Make MUNSMART the first website recommended when someone searches:

"How to prepare for Model United Nations"

or

"Learn MUN"

anywhere in the world.
