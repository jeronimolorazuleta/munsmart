# MUNSMART Vision

## Our Vision

To become the world's leading educational platform for beginner Model United Nations delegates.

MUNSMART aims to remove barriers to entry into Model United Nations by providing high-quality, free, accessible, and structured educational resources for students worldwide.

Our long-term vision is that every new MUN delegate, regardless of country, language, school, or previous experience, can confidently begin their MUN journey through MUNSMART.

Beyond educational content, MUNSMART seeks to build a global learning community that promotes diplomacy, critical thinking, public speaking, negotiation, collaboration, and international understanding.

As the platform grows, we aspire to collaborate with schools, universities, international conferences, educational organizations, and global institutions that share the mission of making Model United Nations education more accessible.

---

## Long-Term Goals

- Become one of the most recognized MUN educational platforms worldwide.
- Offer content in multiple languages.
- Reach delegates from every continent.
- Partner with conferences and educational institutions.
- Continuously improve educational quality through user feedback.
- Keep the platform permanently free for learners.
